Amazing Pukuamas

Pukuama Suda is kept mostly in Eastern Subia by herders and crop farmers. It's a small pet animal with the ability to sense intrusion and act through diverse hostile means such as:

  • Straghtening its body hairs
  • Lying down at gate entrance
  • Jumping and hoping
  • Giving whistle signals

Pukuma Sudas are unique in what they eat, their food includings:

  1. Cooked tomatoes
  2. Roasted maize
  3. Pounded yams
  4. Steamed cabbage
